SERVICES

Program Management consulting services offered:

Project Risk Assessment

Maximize positive results and minimize negative outcomes. Our guidance can assist you on better predicting residual risks and the ripple effect of a project decision.

Project Planning and Launch

Outline a game plan that will address the monitoring, tracking and controlling of the project once it gets under way.

Project Execution

Allow us to oversee the performance of your project scope and acitivites in accordance with the project schedule, design, procedues, resources and safety regulations.

Project Monitoring and Control

Ensure project work is carried out according to the plan and provide guidance on any items necessary to ensure the project goes according to plan.

Change Management

Identify ways to strategically plan for change, functions of critical roles that should be incorporated into plans to quickly adapt and increase likelihood of success.

Project Closure

Assurance that all deliverables were accomplished in accordance to scope of the project and in the appropriate timely manner.

Team Building/ Mentoring

Work with individual contributors to build a strong foundation for effective interpersonal communication. Facilitate the behavioral and communication dynamics essential to a team's collaboration.

Mid-Project Audit

Evaluate existing project plans and project progress reports to identify problem areas and to determine appropriate strategies to refocus, remove roadblocks, and motivate the team with a clear sense of direction.

Problem Intervention

Analyze a current problem situation to determine its cause and related impact to devise appropriate corrective actions.

Post-Project Evaluation

Review and evaluate close out of the project so that lessons learned can be identified as a growth experience for the future.
