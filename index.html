ABOUT US

At Set Boundaries, we specialize in the three most critical phases of project execution—starting right, fixing fast, and closing clean.

Focused on the aerospace and maritime industries, we partner with organizations to protect cost, control schedule, and deliver complex programs with confidence.
Whether you need to launch a high-stakes initiative with a solid execution plan, rescue a troubled program that's off-track, or bring a long-overdue project to a disciplined close, we provide the leadership, technical insight, and operational clarity to make it happen—fast.

What we do Best?

  • Front-End Planning: We set projects up for success by aligning stakeholders, defining scope, and establishing governance from day one.
  • Program Recovery: We step in when programs falter—diagnosing root causes, stabilizing execution, and restoring customer trust in record time.
  • Project Closeout: We bring precision and urgency to lingering projects, ensuring clean delivery, documentation, and performance validation.

With a team of seasoned project engineers, certified program managers (PMP®), and domain experts in both maritime and aerospace systems, we bring structure to chaos and progress to stalled efforts. If you're looking for a partner who thrives under pressure and delivers results where others stall—we’re that team.

SERVICES

Program Management & Project Engineering Services

Built for Aerospace & Maritime Mission Success We deliver structured, fast-acting project leadership across the entire lifecycle—whether you're launching, rescuing, or closing a program. Our services are tailored for complex, high-stakes environments where time, compliance, and performance matter.

Integrated Program Planning

Align people, scope, and budget from day one. We create clear, executable roadmaps that bring together strategy, operations, and engineering to drive mission success.

Microsoft Project Scheduling (DCMA 14-Point Compliant)/p>

Build trustworthy, audit-ready schedules. Real time creation of MS Project schedules that pass the DCMA 14-point check—supporting EVM, critical path, and government reporting standards.

Program Recovery

Fix troubled programs—fast. We diagnose root issues, stabilize operations, and restore your customer’s confidence in record time.

Earned Value Management (EVM) & Project Controls

Track cost and schedule with precision. From performance baselines to SPI/CPI tracking, we provide real-time visibility and control over your project’s financial and schedule health.

Risk & Opportunity Management

Mitigate risk before it impacts delivery. We identify, assess, and manage technical and programmatic risks, keeping your project secure and adaptive.

Scope Control & Technical Baseline Management

Keep engineering on track. We lock in scope, manage changes, and protect the technical baseline from drift—aligned with AS9100 and ISO standards.

Stakeholder & Communication Management

Clarity across all levels. From internal teams to government agencies, we create clear reporting structures and engagement plans that reduce rework and decision lags.

Independent Program Assessments

See the real status—before it's too late. We provide third-party reviews of program performance and contract alignment to help you course-correct or validate delivery confidence.

Project Closeout & Compliance

Wrap up cleanly and completely. We close out stagnant or delayed programs efficiently—securing deliverables, clearing audits, and freeing up resources.

Agile Project Management for Engineering Teams

Speed + structure. We blend Agile principles with traditional controls for R&D and systems teams needing flexibility without losing traceability.
