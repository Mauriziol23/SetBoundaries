SERVICES

Program management consulting services offered

Project Risk Assessment

Maximize the probability and consequences of positive events to project outcomes and minimize the probability and consequences of negative events to project outcomes. Guidance on better predicting residual risks and the ripple effect of a project decision.

Project Planning and Launch

Outline a game plan that will address the monitoring, tracking and controlling of the project once it gets under way.

Project Execution

performing the project scope of works and activities in accordance with the project baselines, plans and procedures with the resource, interface, change, schedule, cost, risk, quality, safety and environment management, contractual requirements, and regulation.

Project Monitoring and Control

Ensure project work is carried out according to the plan and provide guidance on any items necessary to ensure the project goes according to plan.

Change Management

Identify ways to strategically plan for change, functions of critical roles that should be incorporated into plans, recognize how culture can affect change, and plan activities to increase the likelihood of success.

Project Closure

The never-ending project - Assurance that all the work has been completed, that all agreed upon project management processes have been executed, and formal recognition of the completion of a project—everyone agrees that it is completed.

Team Building/ Mentoring

Work with individual contributors to build a strong foundation for effective interpersonal communication. Facilitate the behavioral and communication dynamics essential to a team's collaboration.

Mid-Project Audit

Evaluate existing project plans and project progress reports to identify problem areas and to determine appropriate strategies to refocus, remove roadblocks, and motivate the team with a clear sense of direction..

Problem Intervention

The project is in trouble - Analyze a current problem situation to determine its cause and related impact to devise appropriate corrective actions.

Post-Project Evaluation

Benefit from your teams experience - Review and evaluate a recently completed project so that lessons learned can be identified as a growth experience for the project team and documented for use by future teams.
